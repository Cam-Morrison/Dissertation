What does A.I. assist do?


Stock Price Prediction

Time Series Forecasting

AI assist time series forecasting is a trial feature that utilises regression models from Microsoft's ML.NET code integration in an attempt to aid long term investment strategies by giving a rough estimate of trend based on historical prices. However, historical prices is not an accurate indicator of future performance and AI assist should not be taken as investment advise.


News Sentiment

Natural Language Processing

AI assist sentiment analysis is a trial feature that utilises natural language processing from Microsoft's ML.NET code integration in an attempt to group news articles into positive or negative sentiment. It does this by reading the description of a news article and determining the mood. This feature is designed to improve user experience by allowing a user to avoid negative news or filter the articles to find where the market is prosperous. However, AI assist can be clumsy and try to hard to classify neutral (interpretable) headlines.
